About The Hackathon

Mindsprint2K25 is a 36-hour hackathon organized by Potti Sriramulu Chalavadi Mallikarjuna Rao College of Engineering and Technology. This event brings together innovative minds to collaborate, create, and solve real-world problems through technology.

Participants will have the opportunity to work on exciting projects across various domains, network with industry professionals, and showcase their skills to potential employers and mentors.

Hackathon Domains

AI & ML

Develop innovative solutions using artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms.

IoT, Robotics & Automation

Create smart devices, robots, and automated systems to solve real-world problems.

Women Empowerment

Develop solutions that address challenges faced by women and promote gender equality.

Web3 & Blockchain

Build decentralized applications and explore blockchain technology innovations.

Fintech

Create solutions for financial technology challenges and innovations in banking and finance.

Biomedical

Develop healthcare solutions and biomedical innovations to improve patient care.

Mobile & Web Applications

Build innovative mobile and web applications to solve everyday problems.

Cyber Security

Create solutions to address cybersecurity challenges and protect digital assets.
